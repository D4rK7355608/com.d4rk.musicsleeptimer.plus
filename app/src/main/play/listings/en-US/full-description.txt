How it works
Simply add the Music Sleep Timer Plus notification tile to your Quick Settings bar, tap the notification tile to set a timer for your audio playback and choose from a specific time or duration. Once the timer is set, Music Sleep Timer Plus will automatically turn off your audio at the specified time, ensuring uninterrupted sleep.
